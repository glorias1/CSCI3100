Help Center

In MapOut, project leader can add tasks to a project by clicking the plus (+) sign in the right bottom corner. Task name, due date and responsible members should be filled in. Then you click Add to finish the process. Tasks can be set to finished by the responsible members. Reminder email will be sent to resopnsible members in a certain periods before the due date of the tasks.

For creating a project, first click the + sign in the right bottom corner and select 'Create a project'.
You need to enter the name and description of the project.
The person who created the project will be set as the Projectc leader as default.
Then, the project leader can invite other people to be project members.

The Google calendar is connected if you allow the permission.
You can manage your schedule when the tasks are added.
The event will be created on your calendar accordingly.
